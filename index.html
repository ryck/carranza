Antonio Carranza

3D Artist

Hi, my name is Antonio Carranza and I am a 3D artist with more than 14 years of experience in TV, cinema and advertising. I’m passionate about my profession and I could not imagine myself doing anything else.

About

My first professional experience in the 3D industry took place at Bren Entertainment (Filmax Animation) in Spain. During my eight years there, I had the opportunity to evolve as a 3D artist. This was mainly thanks to working closely with a fantastic team of artists who were willing to share their abilities and experience with me.

At Bren Entertainment I worked on several animation feature films, series and short films. Some of these won great recognition thanks to various awards, including winning Best Animated Movie by the Spanish Academy of Cinematographic Arts and Sciences.

In March 2010, I moved to London and began working for The Mill. To me, this is the best advertising company in the world. I was extremely lucky to work in such an exciting professional environment - every day is a challenge, fuelled by highly-demanding clients and technically complex projects. Being part of The Mill’s family for almost two years was a truly remarkable experience, and I will always be grateful to the amazing team there.

After leaving The Mill, I had the pleasure of working for Sky, one of the biggest TV corporations in the UK. There, I worked is a generalist for the Sky Sports and Formula 1 channels for almost 5 years. However, I also collaborated with other channels upon request.

And now, since December last year and after 7 years living in London, I began working for Double Negative, one of the best film companies in the world, where I'm working is a Lighting TD for the film division.

Feel free to contact me after viewing my website and portfolio, or if you want to share your views and discuss any projects. If you are looking for somebody to fill a position and feel I may be the perfect person for it, please do not hesitate to contact me. I thrive on new challenges.
