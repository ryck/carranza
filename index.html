Antonio Carranza

3D Artist

I have over 14 years of experience in TV series, commercials and feature films (set modelling, character set-up, FX, lighting and compositing). At the moment I work as a 3D artist at BSkyB, but I am always looking for news challenges and I love working in a fun environment with friendly and professional people. If you are interested in my work or and/or you need further information, please do not hesitate to contact me
